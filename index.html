Know Before You Go

LOGISTICS

FLIGHTS

Fly into Salt Lake City International (SLC).

Park City is 35 minutes east on I-80. SLC is a Delta hub with plenty of direct routes.

LODGING

GETTING THERE

Uber from SLC airport to Park City is about 35 minutes.

We will aim to carpool from the airport where possible, so coordinate arrival times in the group chat. Once in Park City, Main Street is walkable from the house.

WEATHER & ELEVATION

Park City sits at 7,000 ft (trails up to 10,000 ft). Late August: 70-85°F days, 45-55°F nights.

Hydrate well, especially if you're coming from sea level. The altitude is no joke.
